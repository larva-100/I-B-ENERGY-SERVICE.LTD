Energy Service .lTD (I): ESCOs are entities that provide energy-related services to end-users.
They often focus on energy efficiency, renewable energy, and demand-side management. Services may include energy audits, energy-efficient technologies, and financing solutions to help clients reduce energy consumption. Energy Efficiency Services:
Energy efficiency services aim to optimize energy consumption and reduce waste.
